GOOGLE SAYS THANKS TO ALL THE HELPERS THROUGH ITS DOODLE : COVID 19A THANK YOU BY GOOGLE IN ITS OWN

The coronavirus has created a havoc in the recent times and the whole world is fighting unitedly against it. People across the world are staying indoors, working from home while schools, colleges and most offices are shut. Yet there are many people who are risking their lives and thriving hard for the best. Today, the doodle that Google shared was a compilation of all those doodles, thanking all the people who are at the forefront fighting Covid-19 . Google here takes a chance in appreciating these people who are helping , guiding , struggling in these difficult times. Google has done it in its own style by doing a beautiful doodle.

These people involve healthcare workers, sanitation workers, farmers, food service workers, the police officials, the firemen, people helping with public transportation, packing, shipping and delivery people as well.This illustration mostly depicted all the people engaged in the essential services who are out of their homes yet without a complaint carrying out their duty in concern with love of the world. It has also illustrated those helpers like teachers and professors who are taking classes, lectures ,demonstrations through video calls in response to avoid educational downfall.

These people involve healthcare workers, sanitation workers, farmers, food service workers, the police officials, the firemen, people helping with public transportation, packing, shipping and delivery people as well. Actually, Google has been doing these doodles and expressing their gratitude through art, but this doodle does not leave behind any of the essential services. We also thank all these people from bottom of our hearts and wish you all a happy quarantine with lots of health and hygiene. Stay safe. Help the world. #google #socioad #digitalmediaupdates
