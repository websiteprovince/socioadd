Are you the one?

We are actively seeking partnership of enterprising individuals who are passionate about the marketing industry. The franchisee appointment will be selectively granted to prospects that demonstrate not only adequate business understanding, but also an impressive business track record, exceptional leadership traits, proven management capabilities and an ability to adapt to a constantly changing and evolving marketing industry.
Individuals of certified marketing skills will be given preference owing to their relevant experience in the industry. However, non-marketing professionals who display an interest in associating with Socio ad, and willingness to acquire a marketing business are encouraged to seek partnership with us. Applicants must also demonstrate sound financial resources to drive business growth without any disruption.

Do I need relevant experience in the marketing industry?

Experience in marketing industry is preferred but is not a show stopper for people who have passion for the this industry, adequate business awareness, leadership skills, proven management capabilities and an ability to adapt to a constantly changing and evolving market is preferred.

What all services does ‘Socio ad’ deal in?

‘Socio ad’ serves customers with Social Media Marketing, Digital Marketing, Website Development, Outdoor Ads, Photo/Video Ads, Business Consultancy Services & Business Start-up services.

Is an office space necessary to start the business?

Offices are opened to cater to the increasing requirements of the customers. Generally, it is seen that customers prefer to purchase the service post a satisfactory and thorough discussion in person with executives at our offices, as compared to making an online meet.

What is the potential for earnings for a non-office franchise?

Franchisee gets a fixed commission percent as per the gross sale amount of the services sold. Maximum number of service sold may earn you up with extra incentives.

Is the franchise fee refundable?

- No, franchise fee is non-refundable and is applicable for a period of 3 years. This agreement is renewable on the basis of the performance of the franchise.

Who will build up my franchise?

The franchise will be responsible for internal communication, branding and project work. ‘Socio ad’ marketing team will assist the franchise to select the location and to maintain the standard of office specifications.

What support would be given by Socio ad?

- Operational Support through business management team
- Marketing support to conduct local activities
- Guidelines for recruitment
- Training and Development Support
- Special services and offers on requirement
- Technological support

Will I get recruitment and training assistance?

‘Socio ad’ assists franchisee’s with guidelines on recruitment of suitable staff. Training is an integral part of Socio ad franchisee business and is done through training programs and regular process & service training workshops.

Is location an important selection criteria?

Yes, location is an important selection criteria and the office should ideally be at a medium to high footfall area, with a good frontage and signage space, preferably at high street, near shopping malls or commercial complexes.
